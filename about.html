About Us

About Developer

Servicio Mobile App was founded by Gerr Jan Manago in January 2022. He is an App Developer and an IT Consultant in different Law Enforcement Agencies in the Philippines. He is also the CEO of Agciotech IT Company and President of Servicio App Incorporated. His passion is to help those unemployed people who were deeply affected by the pandemic and suddenly lost their jobs. He wanted to secure that every Pilipino has a chance to work even if the Pandemic strikes.

App Developer will bring his experience and expertise to help build and grow Servicio App incorporated in the freelancing and professional platform industry in the Philippines.

Product and Services

Servicio App is a freelancing marketplace that connects clients and independent professionals. The client chooses the professionals they want to work with. Professionals can look for work, get hired, and even get paid all within the platform. The platform makes it easy for freelancers to find and work with clients.

There are 60 varieties of skilled service providers to choose from on the Servicio App. Servicio App lists jobs such as Car Mechanic, Electrician, Plumber, Aircon Cleaner, Aircon Installer, Driver, CCTV Installer, House Helper, Towing Services, Caregiver, and many more.


Our Mission and Vision Statement

Mission
Our Mission is to provide people with economic opportunities for a better life.

Vision
To become the leading labor marketplace, where everyday businesses of all sizes and independent talent in the Philippines come together here to accomplish various projects.
