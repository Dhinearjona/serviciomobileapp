About Us

About Developer

Servicio Mobile App was founded by Gerr Jan Manago an App Developer. His mission is to help those unemployed people who were deeply affected by losing their jobs during the pandemic service providers who are skilled and talented, with or without a job are very much welcome to join the app for clients also who wanted to be more secure in looking for service providers then this app is the most convenient way to get to job done.

Servicio App Incorporated connects clients with independent professionals in the Philippines. The application helps professionals find projects, communicate with clients, and get paid.

Our business goal is to create economic opportunities for the Filipino people. The business will be managed by Mr. Gerr Jan A. Manago, App Developer.

He will bring his experience and expertise to help build and grow Servicio App incorporated in the freelancing platform industry in the Philippines.

Product and Services

Servicio App is a freelancing marketplace that connects clients and independent professionals. The client chooses the professionals they want to work with. Professionals can look for work, get hired, and even get paid all within the platform. The platform makes it easy for freelancers to find and work with clients.

There are 60 varieties of skilled service providers to choose from on the Servicio App. Servicio App lists jobs such as Car Mechanic, Electrician, Plumber, Aircon Cleaner, Aircon Installer, Driver, CCTV Installer, House Helper, Towing Services, Caregiver, and many more.
